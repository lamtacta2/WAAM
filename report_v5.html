1. Problem description

Among metallic additive manufacturing technologies, wire and arc-directed energy deposition (WADED) is recently adopted to manufacture large industrial components. In this process, controlling the temperature evolution is very important since it directly influences the quality of the deposited parts. Consequently, we develop a simple GUI-based Virtual Lab to visualize the results of the WADED process. This software aims to give students and researchers the most simple, and comprehensive scientific overview of the aforementioned phenomenon.
